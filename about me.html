Roman civilization

The ancient Romans built one of the greatest empires in world history. However, whereas the Roman Empire lasted from about 30 BCE to 476 CE, Ancient Roman civilization emerged long before, in the centuries after 800 BCE. This article deals primarily with Roman life and culture.
